LawBreaker Safety Issues

Rush Yellow Light but Stop at Junction.

In this situation, the traffic light turns from green to yellow at 10S and the ego vehicle speed up to rush the yellow light. However, the ego vehicle stop at the junction at 14S even when there is no NPC vehicle ahead and cause collision with the NPC vehicle at left 16S. Hence, the ego vehicle violates the yellow light parts of Article38.

Overtake from another road and Cause Collisions.

As can been seen form the video, the ego vehicle chooses to overtake from the lane with opposite direction 3S even when there is possible pass route on the right lane, and cause collision when returning to the original lane (12S). Hence, the ego vehicle violates Article44 and Article47.
