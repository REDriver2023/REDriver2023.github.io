Hesitate at Yellow Light and Cross Junction at Red Light-Case1.

In this situation, the traffic light turns from green to yellow at 12S and there is not enough distance for the vehicle to stop. Although the ego vehicle is expected to go straight, it hesitated at the junction (14S) and crossed the intersection at the red light (20S). Hence, the ego vehicle violates the yellow light and red light parts of Article38.

Hesitate at Yellow Light and Cross Junction at Red Light-Case2.

In this situation, the traffic light turns from green to yellow at 9S and the ego vehicle has reached the stop line. Although the ego vehicle is expected to go straight, it hesitated at the junction (11S) and crossed the intersection at the red light (18S). Hence, the ego vehicle violates the yellow light and red light parts of Article38.
