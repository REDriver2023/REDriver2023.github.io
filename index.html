Rush Yellow Light and Cause Accidents-Case1.

In this situation, the traffic light turns from green to yellow at 10S and there is enough distance for the vehicle to stop. However, the ego vehicle fails to stop before the stop line and decide to rush the yellow light and cause collision with the NPC vehicle ahead at 13S. The behavior of rush yellow light causes accidents since there is a speed up when the vehicle is close to the stop line and the ego vehicle violates the yellow light related part of Article38.
