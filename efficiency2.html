Fail to Overtake the Static Vehicle Ahead at Intersection.

In this situation, there is a static NPC vehicle ahead and the traffic light is green (20S). The ego vehicle is expected to overtake the static NPC vehicle. However, the ego vehicle gets stuck and fail to overtake (23S). Hence, the ego vehicle violates the green light part of Article38.
